USE CASE

Supply Chain on Blockchain

Smart packaging solutions for product authentication and supply chain traceability.

Involved parties

ScanTrust delivers its smart QR-code services to the world's largest companies, such as ExxonMobil, Unilever, Shell, and AB InBev.

Business challenges

Counterfeiting of QR codes on products

End customers do not have the tools to trust the product information presented to them to verify the goods purchased are counterfeit, damaged or misrepresented

Collaboration between stakeholders

Third-party supplier or distribution partners with administrative access rights to its supply chain management system.

Integrity of supply chain systems

Tampering of data can often go undetected by any of the involved parties in a supply chain and therefore risk the integrity of the system

Solution overview

Tamperproof QR codes

Generating QR codes that contain a technology that is used to sign the submissions of each data entry and report being made in LTO Network to create an immutable record.

Effective collaboration

Improvement of efficiency by digitizing the paper-based processes and change in communication between the different businesses, reducing human errors when collaborating on supply chain workflows.

Data sharing

Data that is shared by the parties involved using the systems connected to LTO Network. Stakeholders can benefit from data re-use and reduction of manual process handling.

Advantages using LTO Network

Cost savings

Administrative tasks such as manual entering data into your system that receive by letter, fax, or email belongs to the past. With the blockchain technology of LTO Network, all parties can collect structured data into the systems they use to execute and administrate these processes. Working this way reduces the administrative overhead with more than 30%.

Easy integration

With LTO Network, you don't have to change your entire IT infrastructure. You can use your existing systems and application to administrate, record, and communicate on business processes.

Compliant by design

Demonstrating compliance is essential in any industry. Using LTO Network you can record all data entries and steps in your business processes to safeguarding evidence and comply with the regulations. Enabling one-click compliance reporting for both internal- as external communication on your business processes.
