Involved parties

Stakeholders in the waste management value chain in Europe

Business challenges

Numerous stakeholders

At the moment, multiple different government bodies supervise and authorize the export, import and transportation of company waste throughout the European Union.

Existing systems

The EU supervises various aspects of these processes such as granting permits, notifying involved parties of waste transports and monitoring financial security in existing systems.

Tons of paperwork

These processes involve a lot of work that is still mostly done by paper and regular mail. The ministry of ILT wanted to streamline this process, and ideally go paperless while doing so.
