USE CASE

Document Management on Blockchain

Document management systems can be used for any type of document. Adding blockchain helps with authenticating everything that takes place in a DMS.

Involved parties

Enterprise clients

Business challenges

Evidence of date and time

In case there is a dispute about when and at what the exact time a document was uploaded, you need to be able to verify this reliably.

Document authenticity

In case there is a dispute about which document and it's content was uploaded, you need to be able to validate which one is the original one.

Document verification

Documents that are shared and used by multiple parties need to be trusted by everyone involved, and independently verifiable.

Solution overview

Immutable files

A document can be represented as a digital fingerprint by 'hashing' the document. LTO Network stores the hash of this document through a with LTO Network connected systems Anyone with access to the data can verify the document by hashing it and comparing it against the stored version.

Verifiable timestamp

Not only the hashed document (fingerprint) is stored on the blockchain but also the timestamp of the document. By making documents verifiable and independently auditable, they become more valuable. LTO Network creates portable proof of your timestamped claims. These blockchain-based verifications can be shared and transferred to other parties.

No need for third party verification

Using the on open standards-based LTO Network is, any party can independently verify the authenticity and timestamp of the document, without having to rely on a trusted third party.

Advantages using LTO Network

Protection against claims

The evidence that a document has been uploaded by a person in the data room or other document management system is indisputable. This protects your organization against potential claims.

Easy integration

With LTO Network, you don't have to change your entire IT infrastructure. You can use your existing systems and applications to administer, record, and communicate with LTO Network. The implementation of LTO Network is as simple as connecting an API.

Data governance

Documents uploaded and the blockchain receipt with the details of the upload can never be altered. Therefore, you have a foolproof record of everything that goes in and out of your dataroom. LTO Network combined with Virtual Vaults makes it easy to manage who has access and whether documents where viewed, downloaded or removed.
