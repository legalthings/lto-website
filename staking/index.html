Staking on LTO Network

LTO Network’s public blockchain is PoS based - Fair Leased Proof of Stake. Anyone can become a validator on mainnet and share the non-inflationary network rewards, supporting the next wave of adoption!

You can set up your own node or lease to a community node. Your APR depends on your stake share and the transactions in the network.
